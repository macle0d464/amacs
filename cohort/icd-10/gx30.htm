Other external causes of accidental injury
(W00-X59)

Exposure to forces of nature
(X30-X39)

[See at the beginning of this chapter for fourth-character subdivisions]

X30 Exposure to excessive natural heat
[See pages 1013-1017 for fourth-character subdivisions]
Includes: excessive heat as the cause of sunstroke
exposure to heat NOS
Excludes: excessive heat of man-made origin ( W92.- )

X31 Exposure to excessive natural cold
[See pages 1013-1017 for fourth-character subdivisions]
Includes: excessive cold as the cause of:
· chilblains NOS
· immersion foot or hand
exposure to:
· cold NOS
· weather conditions
Excludes: cold of man-made origin ( W93.- )
contact with or inhalation of:
· dry ice ( W93.- )
· liquefied gas ( W93.- )

X37 Victim of cataclysmic storm
[See pages 1013-1017 for fourth-character subdivisions]
Includes: blizzard
cloudburst
cyclone
hurricane

Excludes: collapse of dam or man-made structure causing earth movement ( X36.- )

X38 Victim of flood
[See pages 1013-1017 for fourth-character subdivisions]
Includes: flood:
· arising from remote storm
· of cataclysmic nature arising from melting snow
· resulting directly from storm
Excludes: collapse of dam or man-made structure causing earth movement ( X36.- )
tidal wave:
· NOS ( X39.- )
· caused by storm ( X37.- )
