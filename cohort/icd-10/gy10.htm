Chapter XX
External causes of morbidity and mortality
(V01-Y98)

Event of undetermined intent
(Y10-Y34)

[See at the beginning of this chapter for fourth-character subdivisions]
Note: This section covers events where available information is insufficient to enable a medical or legal authority to make a distinction between accident, self-harm and assault. It includes self-inflicted injuries, but not poisoning, when not specified whether accidental or with intent to harm.

[See pages 1013-1017 for fourth-character subdivisions]

pyrazolone derivatives
salicylates
